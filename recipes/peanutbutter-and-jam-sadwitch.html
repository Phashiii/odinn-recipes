Classic Peanutbutter and Jam Sandwitch

Description

This is the absolute perfect sadwitch. It only requires 3 ingredients and can be made in under a minute.

Ingredients

  • Bread(Any bread of choice will do just fine)
  • Jam(Your favourite flavour ofcourse)
  • Peanutbutter(I prefer the chunky kind but smooth will do if that's more your preference)

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Place a slice of bread on a clean flat surface.

  2. Step 2

    3. Dip a knife or spoon into the peanutbutter.

  3. Step 3

    4. Spread the peanutbutter on the slice of bread. Dip into the peanutbutter as much as required to ceate a thick layer over the slice of bread.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place another slice of bread on a clean flat surface.

  5. Step 5

    6. Clean the knife or spoon used in the preius steps and dip it into the jam.

  6. Step 6

    7. Spread the jam on the slice of bread as thickly and envenly as possible. Dip into the jam as much as neccessary to get the desired texture.

  7. Step 7

    8. Combine the two slices into a sandwitch and ENJOY!
