French Toats

Description

This is a classic known in many names all around the world. It is a staple breakfast option in my household and it has been that way for as long as I can remember. Quick and easy to make and it requires staple items which I am sure most of us already include on our shopping lists.

Ingredients

  • Bread(Any bread will do just as long as it is thick)
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Oil (Any kitchen oil of your choice will work)

    • Optional

  • Cinnamon

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Mix 3 eggs, a teaspoon of salt, a half a teaspoon of sugar, half a cup of milk and cinnamon together in a bowl.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a frying pan on medium heat.

  3. Step 3

    4. Once the oil is sufficiently hot, dip a slice of bread into the egg mix ensuring both sides are coverd and place it into the pan.

  4. Step 4

    5. After 2 min flip the bread and fry the other side. Repeat this until both sides are cooked through.

  5. Step 5

    6. Once you are satisfied with the cook fo the sice repeat steps 3 to 5 for the rest of the bread.

  6. Step 6

    7. Enjoy!
