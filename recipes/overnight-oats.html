Overnight Oats

Description

This is an easy oats recipe that is sure to get your day started out just right. The best part is all the ingredients required to make this delicious delight are probably in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Oats(Steel-cut or old fashioned)
  • Milk(Dairy or plant based)
  • Cinnamon
  • Frozen mixed berries
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. First you will need to measure about a cup of oats and pour it in to a bowl.

  2. Step 2

    3. Next pour in 2 cups of milk or dairy alternitive of your choice. Make sure it covers the oats fully.

  3. Step 3

    4. Mash the banana and mixed berries into the oats and thoroughly mix them unitl the form a thick creamy texture.

  4. Step 4

    5. Add a half teaspoon of cinnamon and put the oats in the fridge. Keep in there for atleast 4 hours to an hours so that the milk and flavour can be absorbed by the oats.

  5. Step 5

    6. Enjoy!
